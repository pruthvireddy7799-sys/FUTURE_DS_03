# 📊 Bank Marketing Analysis Dashboard

## 🎯 Objective

The objective of this project is to analyze bank customer data and identify patterns that influence whether a customer subscribes to a term deposit.

---

## 📊 Dashboard Features

* Total Customers (KPI)
* Subscribed Customers (KPI)
* Subscription Rate (KPI)
* Subscription Distribution (Yes vs No)
* Subscription by Job
* Subscription by Age (binned)
* Subscription by Balance (binned)
* Subscription by Contact Method
* Subscription by Education
* Subscription by Marital Status

---

## 💡 Key Insights

* Customers aged **30–50** are more likely to subscribe
* Customers contacted via **cellular** have higher conversion rates
* Customers with **higher account balances** show higher subscription probability
* Job roles like **management and technician** show better response rates
